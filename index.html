World Travel Awards (WTA). South America 2015.

Recognized as the city ranked as best destination in Colombia, the best getaway destination, the best Travel destination and the best Tourism Promoting City (2015).

Awards

Good Practices Award by the UN. Culture and Respect with LGBTI population.

In 2015 UN Habitat along with the government of Dubai, bestowed upon Bogota this award because of it's public policy of 'In Bogota you can be' a strategy that looks to encourage a culture of respect towards LGBTI groups.

Pride Flag

City of Music. Awarded by UNESCO.

In 2012, UNESCO recognized Bogota as a city which promotes music as a tool in the socioeconomic and cultural diversity development. It was also a recognition to the growth in its musical sector and the central stage of music creation of Latin America.
